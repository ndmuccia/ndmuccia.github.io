This work was done during the two—week Werkplaats Typografie ISIA Urbino Summer School 2018. The theme this year was 'rituals'. Throughout this project I was concerned with ritualistic manifestations of supersitious dread. The project focuses on superstitions and their potential to effect the way we navigate through space. After finding out some local superstitions held by the people of Urbino (and creating some of my own), I wanted to bring a hyperawareness to them. Just as traffic and municipal signs map a space and give collective instruction (with consequences!), superstitions map and instruct myth—based disruptions. . .
